INSTRUCTIONS:

If you don't know how Guess Who works, you will need 2 people in order to play. Ask a friend to get the game on their device, and play through a call or just side by side. Each player needs their own game and their own screen.

Once you're set, take turns asking "yes" or "no" questions to try and figure out who your opponent's character is. Whoever guesses correct first wins.

Here are some RULES to spice up the game. You can adjust them to your liking.

  1. No repeated questions. Once a question is asked, it can't be asked again.
  2. No questions about the card's placement on the board.
  3. Each player gets 3 guesses. Guessing also takes a turn.
  4. Optional rule: allow one final guess after losing so the round can end on a tie.

As a fun HARD MODE alternative, try a turn where you're only allowed to ask subjective questions instead of pure yes/no ones.
